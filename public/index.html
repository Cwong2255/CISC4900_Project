Enroll in CISC 4900

CISC 4900 enrollment requires department permission for registration. Please contact Prof. Katherine Chuang or Prof. Yedidyah Langsam regarding enrollment for CISC 4900.

*Permission is only granted to those who are graduating in the upcoming semester, barring exceptional circumstances.

Internship

Students may use projects at work for their CISC 4900 project. Internships typically applying far in advance of seeking enrollment. Please refer below for opportunities.
